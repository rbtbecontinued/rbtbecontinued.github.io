About Me

I am now working toward my Ph.D. degree with the School of Electrical Engineering and Telecommunications, UNSW, Sydney. My background is in communications and signal processing. Prior to I starting my Ph.D., I received the M.Eng.Sc. degree from Fudan University, Shanghai in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Xin Wang. I was also a Researcher with the Radar Research AVIC, Beijing from July 2015 to June 2018, and an Algorithm Engineer with the Ride-Hailing Business Unit, Meituan, Beijing from June 2018 to August 2021.

My current research interests lie in signal processing, machine learning, and graph theory.

I am fortunate to be advised by A/Prof. Vidhyasaharan Sethu, Dr. Ting Dang, and A/Prof. Beena Ahmed.
