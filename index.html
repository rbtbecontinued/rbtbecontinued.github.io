About Me

I am now working toward my Ph.D. degree with the School of Electrical Engineering and Telecommunications, UNSW, Sydney.
My background is in communications and signal processing. I received the M.Eng.Sc. degree from Fudan University, Shanghai
in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Xin Wang. Before I started my Ph.D., I was a Researcher with the Radar Research AVIC,
Beijing (2015–2018), and an Algorithm Engineer with the Ride-Hailing Business Unit, Meituan, Beijing (2018–2021).

My current research interests lie in signal processing, machine learning, and graph theory.

I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Vidhyasaharan Sethu, Dr. Ting Dang, and Dr. Beena Ahmed.
